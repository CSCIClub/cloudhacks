Frequently Asked Questions



Are there travel reimbursements?

At this time, we are not providing travel reimbursements. If you sign up, please make all efforts to attend!

When should I arrive?

Check-ins will begin from 11 AM on Saturday. Schedule with more details will be released soon

Who is invited?

Any current college or university students are allowed to attend.

What if I am a beginner

No worries at all. Hackathon is a great place to get some experience. We will have mentors, vaious workshops during the hackathon to help you along the way of perfection. We will also have a Slack team where you can ask questions and get help during the hackathon.

Is there place to sleep?

We will have a designated sleeping area, and you are encouraged to bring sleeping bags, blankets, pillows, toiletries, and anything else you need to sleep.

How much does it cost?

CloudHacks hackathon is completely free for eligible and registered students. We will provide you with the venue, food, drinks, caffeine, place to sleep if needed, internet connection, etc

How does teams work?

There is a maximum of 4 people per team. You can come with a premade team, or come alone and we will match you up with other participants during team building activities.

What should I bring?

You will definitely need your laptop, phone, their chargers, as well as a valid student or government ID. Feel free to also bring along a sleeping bag, blankets, toiletries and we recommend some comfortable clothes.

Will there be food and drinks?

Yes! Absolutely! We will provide catered meals, drinks at all times, snack breaks, and endless caffeine.

Is there a code of conduct?

Yes, we abide by the SCSU Code of Conduct.
